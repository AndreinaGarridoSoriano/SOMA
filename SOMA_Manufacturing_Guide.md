# SOMA — Manufacturing & Lab Partner Guide
**April 2026 — Madrid x London**

## 1. What We're Making

SOMA is a liquid concentrate that adds flavour, electrolytes, vitamins, and botanical ingredients to water. The format is a 30 mL squeezable bottle (like Buoy) — one squeeze = one serving.

- **2 SKUs at launch:** LEMON (Hydrate + Soothe) and BERRY (Hydrate + Antioxidants)
- **Target price:** EUR 15-20 per bottle (EUR 0.50-0.70 per serving)
- **Target audience:** Health-conscious 22-40, urban Spain and UK
- **Shelf life:** 12 months minimum
- **Full formula:** See `SOMA_Formulation.md`

## 3. Technical Specs for Manufacturer

### Product
- Type: Liquid concentrate (food supplement)
- Format: 30 mL squeezable bottle, ~30 doses
- Dosing: ~1 mL per squeeze (no measuring needed)
- Sugar-free, no artificial colours
- Clean label approach

### Formula (V1.5) — Per Serving (~1 mL)

**Base:** Purified Water, Natural Fruit Extract, Citric Acid, Malic Acid

**Electrolytes:**
- Sodium (Sodium Citrate): 150-200 mg
- Potassium (Potassium Citrate): 80-100 mg
- Magnesium (Magnesium Citrate): 20-30 mg

**Vitamins:**
- Vitamin C: 40-80 mg
- Vitamin B3 (Niacin): 4-8 mg
- Vitamin B5 (Pantothenic Acid): 1.5-3 mg
- Vitamin B6: 0.5-1 mg
- Vitamin B12: 1-2 ug
- Vitamin D3 (micellized/water-soluble form): 5-10 ug

**Mineral:**
- Zinc (Zinc Citrate): 1.5-2.5 mg

**Functional (varies by SKU):**
- Lemon: Aloe Vera Extract (20-50 mg)
- Berry: Acerola Extract (15-30 mg) + Blueberry Extract (15-30 mg)

**Sweetener:** Stevia (Reb A) — trace
**Preservative:** Potassium Sorbate or natural alternative (e.g., rosemary extract)

### Packaging
- Bottle: 30 mL soft-touch squeezable plastic (HDPE or LDPE)
- Cap: Squirt/squeeze nozzle (not dropper)
- Cap colour: customizable per SKU (yellow for Lemon, pink for Berry)
- Finish: Matte preferred
- Label: We provide the design, lab prints and applies
- Inspiration: Buoy format

## 5. Questions for Lab Meetings

### A. Capacity & Experience
1. Have you made liquid concentrate drops / water enhancers before?
2. Have you worked with squeeze bottle format (not droppers)?
3. Do you handle formulation + filling + labelling, or just formulation?
4. What is your MOQ per SKU?
5. What is the total lead time (formulation to finished product)?

### B. Formulation
6. Can you develop from our ingredient specs and dose ranges?
7. Is it viable to fit all these actives in 1 mL of concentrate?
8. Is micellized/water-soluble Vitamin D3 available for this format?
9. Can Zinc Citrate remain stable in an acidic liquid base long-term?
10. How do you handle flavour development? Tasting samples?
11. How many reformulation rounds are included?
12. Can we do a comparison sample: with vs without B3+B5 for taste?

### C. Stability
13. Do you include stability testing? Protocol and duration?
14. Are vitamins (especially D3, B12) stable in liquid for 12 months?
15. What preservative system do you recommend?

### D. Regulation
16. Do you help with AESAN notification, or do we need a separate consultant?
17. Is the product classified as food supplement or food?
18. Are all our proposed doses within EU limits?
19. Additional registration needed for UK (FSA)?
20. Which EFSA health claims can we legally use?

### E. Packaging
21. Can you fill in squeeze/squirt bottles (not droppers)?
22. Material recommendation for squeezable matte bottle? (HDPE, LDPE?)
23. Do you source bottles/caps or do we supply them?
24. Can cap colour be customized per SKU?

### F. Costs & Logistics
25. Estimated cost per unit for 1,000-2,000 units per SKU?
26. Does the price include: formulation + raw materials + filling + labelling?
27. Any one-time setup/development fees (NRE)?
28. Cost of stability testing?
29. Where are you located? Can you ship to Spain and UK?
30. Payment terms?

### G. Flexibility & Scale
31. Can you do small recurring production runs (monthly)?
32. Lead time for reorders?
33. What is your monthly production capacity?
34. We plan a V2 Pro in 12-18 months with additional ingredients (L-Theanine, Hyaluronic Acid, Coconut Water). Can you formulate that?

## 6. Non-Negotiable Requirements

- **Squeeze/squirt format** — core to the brand. If only droppers, wrong partner.
- **1 mL per serving** — the simplicity of "one squeeze" IS the product.
- **Zero sugar, zero artificial colours** — Stevia only.
- **12-month minimum shelf life** — needed for retail and logistics.
- **Real electrolyte doses** — not symbolic amounts. Functional doses.

---

## 7. Red Flags

- "We only do powders/capsules" → Wrong partner.
- "MOQ is 10,000+ units" → Too risky for launch.
- "We can't do stability testing" → Unacceptable.
- "We only produce 2-3 large batches per year" → No iteration capability.
- Won't give estimated unit cost → Lack of transparency.
