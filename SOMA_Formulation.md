## What we added vs. the original V1 (and why)

| Ingredient | Why it was excluded before | Why we're adding it |
|---|---|---|
| Vitamin D3 | "Poor solubility in water-based drops" | Available in micellized/water-soluble form. Huge marketing differentiator — 80% of Europeans are deficient. Almost no water enhancer includes it. |
| Zinc Citrate | "Off-taste and formulation complexity" | At 1.5-2.5 mg, perfectly soluble in acidic pH (citric + malic acid base). Immune + skin health story. |
| Vitamin B3 (Niacin) | "Too many vitamins create bitterness" | At 4-8 mg, negligible taste impact. Adds EFSA-approved fatigue claim. |
| Vitamin B5 (Pantothenic Acid) | "Too many vitamins create bitterness" | At 1.5-3 mg, negligible taste impact. Adds mental performance claim. |

## What we deliberately excluded (and why)

| Ingredient | Reason for exclusion |
|---|---|
| Calcium Citrate | Minimal benefit at achievable doses (50-80 mg vs 800 mg RDA). Creates turbidity and chalky texture. Save for V2 Pro. |
| Spirulina | Strong green colour and algae taste. Incompatible with clear water aesthetic. Save for SOMA GREEN SKU. |
| Sea Salt / Chloride | Adds complexity without clear consumer benefit vs. sodium citrate. Consider for V2 Pro. |
| L-Theanine | Strong ingredient, but better as hero of a dedicated SOMA FOCUS SKU. |
| Hyaluronic Acid | Excellent differentiator, but save for SOMA GLOW SKU to maximize storytelling. |
| Ashwagandha | Bitter/earthy taste, hard to formulate in 1 mL. Better in dedicated SOMA CALM SKU. |
| Clear Whey | Protein denatures in liquid concentrate, creates bitterness, shortens shelf life. Not viable. |
